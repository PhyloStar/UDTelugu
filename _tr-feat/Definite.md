Definiteness is typically a feature of determiners.
Its value distinguishes whether we are talking about something known and concrete, or something general or unknown.

### `Ind`: indefinite

#### Examples

* _bir_ "a/an"
* _bazı_ "some"
* _birkaç_ "a few/several"

### `Def`: definite

#### Examples

* _bu_ "this"
* _bütün_ "all"
* _her_ "all/each"
