An auxiliary of a clause is a non-main verb of the clause, e.g. modal verbs, "Có_thể" and "phải" in
a composed tense.

<pre><code class="language-sdparse">Tôi phải đi 。
aux(đi, phải)
nsubj(đi, Tôi)
punct(đi, 。)
</code></pre>
