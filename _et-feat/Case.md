Definition


Case is an inflectional feature for [nouns](u-pos/NOUN), [pronouns](u-pos/PRON), [adjectives](u-pos/ADJ) and [numerals](u-pos/NUM) in Estonian.<br/>
Estonian has 14 inflectional cases:<br/>
nominative<br/>
genitive<br/>
partitive<br/>
illative<br/>
inessive<br/>
elative<br/>
allative<br/>
adessive<br/>
ablative<br/>
translative<br/>
terminative<br/>
essive<br/>
abessive<br/>
comitative
