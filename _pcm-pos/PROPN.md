### Definition

A proper noun is a noun (or nominal content word) that is the name (or part of the name) of a specific individual, place, or object.

See [NOUN]() for nouns and [PRON]() for pronouns.

### Examples in Naija : 

- Right now we dey <b>Ifesinachi</b> park for <b>Dugbe</b> for <b>Ibadan</b>.
- Wey be sey una comot go high <b>saturday</b>, <b>friday</b> night go party, go club.
- Im go come reach <b>Ogere</b> now.
- Before im reach <b>Nigeria</b> di whole sea wata corrosion everything don affect di tire.
- You go enter <b>Lagos Ibadan</b> expressway.
